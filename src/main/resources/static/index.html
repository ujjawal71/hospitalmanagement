Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about signing in, booking, and who can use what.

Do I need to install an app?
No. Open the website in Chrome, Edge, or Safari. Staff and patients use the same site with different logins.
How do patients register?
Use the Register page to create an account with name, email, and password. Then sign in as Patient to book.
How do doctors and admins sign in?
Go to Login, choose the Doctor or Admin tab, and use the credentials provided by your hospital (or the default demo accounts in the README).
Can I cancel an appointment?
Yes. From your patient appointments list you can cancel when the status allows, subject to your hospital’s rules.
Where is the hospital located?
This demo highlights Patna, Bihar — see the map in the footer for directions.
