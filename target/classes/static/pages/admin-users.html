How to use this page

Every registered patient can log in and book visits. Accurate contact details reduce no-shows and help staff reach people when schedules change.

  • Edit updates name, email, phone, and optionally sets a new password; leave password blank to keep the current one.
  • Keep email unique where your policy requires it—duplicate accounts confuse booking history.
  • Phone supports reminders and day-of coordination; fill it when patients provide a mobile number.
  • Delete removes the patient from the system—confirm against retention rules before removing someone with past appointments.
